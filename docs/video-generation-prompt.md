# AI Video Generation Prompt for HaLomeid (הלומד)

## Video Overview
**Duration:** 60-90 seconds  
**Style:** Calm, warm, educational, inspirational  
**Tone:** Peaceful, encouraging, non-pressuring  
**Language:** Hebrew (with optional English subtitles)

---

## Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: Opening (0-5 seconds)
**Visual:** Soft fade-in from warm desert tones (beige, cream, soft orange). The mascot (sheep) appears gently, in "peaceful" or "happy" mood, looking directly at the camera with a warm, welcoming expression.

**Text Overlay (Hebrew):**
"הלומד - דרך הלימוד שלך"

**Voiceover (Hebrew):**
"ברוכים הבאים להלומד - אפליקציה ללימוד יומי של המשנה"

**Mascot Action:** Gentle nod or small wave, friendly smile

---

### Scene 2: The Problem (5-15 seconds)
**Visual:** Mascot transitions to "thinking" mood. Soft background showing abstract representations of:
- Overwhelming piles of books
- Confused faces
- Clock ticking (representing time pressure)

**Text Overlay:**
"לימוד תורה יכול להיות מאתגר... אבל לא חייב להיות"

**Mascot Action:** Thoughtful expression, then a reassuring gesture

---

### Scene 3: The Solution - Daily Learning (15-30 seconds)
**Visual:** Mascot transitions to "reading" mood. Show a beautiful, clean learning path visualization:
- A gentle timeline with nodes
- One node highlighted (today's learning)

**Text Overlay:**
"לימוד יומי קטן • דרך מובנית • בקצב שלך"

## Visual Style Guidelines

### Color Palette (Desert Oasis Theme)
- **Primary Background:** Warm beige/cream (#FEFAE0)
- **Secondary Background:** Soft green (#E9EDC9)
- **Accent:** Warm brown/orange (#D4A373)
- **Muted Accent:** Soft sage (#CCD5AE)
- **Text:** Dark brown/charcoal for Hebrew text

### Animation Style
- **Smooth, gentle transitions** - no jarring movements
- **Soft fades** between scenes
- **Natural, organic motion** - like a calm breeze
- **Mascot movements:** Subtle, warm, encouraging
- **Text animations:** Fade in from right (RTL), gentle

### Typography
- **Hebrew Text:** Use a clear, readable Hebrew font (Noto Sans Hebrew or similar)
- **Size:** Large enough to read comfortably
- **Position:** Center or right-aligned (RTL)
- **Style:** Clean, modern, warm

## Full Prompt for AI Video Tool

**Use this as the main prompt:**

Create a 60-90 second promotional video for "HaLomeid" (הלומד), a daily Torah/Mishnah learning app. The video features a friendly sheep mascot (uploaded separately) as the main character guiding viewers through the app's features.

**Visual Style:** Warm desert oasis theme with beige, cream, soft orange, and sage green colors. Smooth, gentle animations with soft fades between scenes. The atmosphere should be calm, peaceful, and encouraging - never rushed or pressuring.

**Story Flow:**
1. Opening: Mascot welcomes viewers warmly (peaceful/happy mood)
2. Problem: Shows how Torah learning can be overwhelming (thinking mood)
3. Solution: Daily structured learning path (reading mood)
4. Features: AI explanations based on classical commentaries (encouraging mood)
5. Progress: Spaced repetition, streaks, celebrations (celebrating mood)
6. Accessibility: Offline-first, works anywhere (peaceful mood)
7. Call to action: Invitation to start the journey (happy/encouraging mood)

**Mascot Behavior:** The mascot should be friendly, warm, and non-judgmental. It acts as a gentle guide, not a teacher. Use different moods (peaceful, thinking, reading, encouraging, celebrating, happy) appropriately throughout the video. Movements should be subtle and natural - gentle nods, warm smiles, encouraging gestures.

**Text Overlays:** Hebrew text (RTL) should appear at key moments with clear, readable fonts. Include: "הלומד - דרך הלימוד שלך", feature highlights, and call-to-action text.

**Voiceover:** Warm, friendly Hebrew voice speaking calmly and encouragingly about the app's benefits. Emphasize: "A clear, calm, and consistent daily learning experience - without overload, guilt, or enforcement."

**Background Music:** Calm, peaceful instrumental music (soft piano or strings) with optional Middle Eastern influences. Low volume, supporting but not distracting.

**Key Messages:**
- Daily structured learning (one mishna per day)
- AI explanations based on classical commentaries
- Spaced repetition for long-term retention
- Progress tracking and celebrations
- Offline-first, works anywhere
- Your pace, your way

**Tone:** Respectful of Jewish learning traditions, welcoming to all levels, emphasizes personal journey over competition. Professional but warm, accessible and inclusive.

The video should feel like a gentle invitation to start a meaningful learning journey, not a sales pitch. The mascot should be the friendly companion on this journey.
