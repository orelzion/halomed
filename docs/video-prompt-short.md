# Short Video Prompt for AI Video Tool

## Direct Prompt

Create a 60-90 second promotional video for "HaLomeid" (הלומד), a daily Torah/Mishnah learning app. 

**Main Character:** A friendly sheep mascot (uploaded separately) guides viewers through the app.

**Visual Style:** Warm desert oasis theme - beige, cream, soft orange, sage green. Smooth, gentle animations. Calm, peaceful, encouraging atmosphere.

**Story (7 scenes, 10-15 seconds each):**

1. **Opening:** Mascot (peaceful/happy) welcomes viewers. Text: "הלומד - דרך הלימוד שלך". Warm fade-in.

2. **Problem:** Mascot (thinking) shows Torah learning can be overwhelming. Text: "לימוד תורה יכול להיות מאתגר... אבל לא חייב להיות".

3. **Solution:** Mascot (reading) shows daily structured learning path - one mishna per day, beautiful timeline. Text: "לימוד יומי קטן • דרך מובנית • בקצב שלך".

4. **AI Features:** Mascot (encouraging) shows Hebrew Mishnah text with AI explanations based on classical commentaries. Text: "הסברים ברורים • מבוססים על פרשנים קלאסיים".

5. **Progress:** Mascot (celebrating) shows progress bars, streaks, celebrations. Text: "חזרה מרווחת • מעקב התקדמות • חגיגות הישגים".

6. **Accessibility:** Mascot (peaceful) shows offline functionality, dark mode, PWA. Text: "עובד גם בלי אינטרנט • מותאם אישית".

7. **Call to Action:** Mascot (happy/encouraging) final invitation. Text: "התחל את המסע שלך היום" + "halomed.vercel.app".

**Mascot Moods (in order):** Peaceful/Happy → Thinking → Reading → Encouraging → Celebrating → Peaceful → Happy/Encouraging

**Mascot Behavior:** Friendly, warm, non-judgmental. Gentle guide, not teacher. Subtle movements: nods, smiles, encouraging gestures, reading motions, celebrations, peaceful sitting.

**Text Style:** Hebrew (RTL), clear readable font, center/right-aligned, fade in from right, dark brown/charcoal color.

**Background Music:** Calm instrumental (soft piano/strings), low volume, optional Middle Eastern influences.

**Voiceover:** Warm Hebrew voice, calm and encouraging. Key message: "A clear, calm, and consistent daily learning experience - without overload, guilt, or enforcement."

**Tone:** Respectful, welcoming, personal journey focus. Gentle invitation, not sales pitch. Professional but warm.

**Key Features to Show:**
- Daily structured learning (one mishna per day)
- AI explanations from classical commentaries
- Spaced repetition system
- Progress tracking and celebrations
- Offline-first functionality
- Your pace, your way

**Resolution:** 1920x1080 or 1080x1920, 30fps, 60-90 seconds.
